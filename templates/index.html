Welcome to Catch Your Feelings!

How it works

Emotions can be broken down into two parts

Low valence Mid valence High valence

Valence: how pleasent an emotion is

Low energy Mid energy High energy

Energy: how strong an emotion is

Examples

High valence High energy Excited
Low valence Low energy Sad
High valence Low energy Relaxed

Food for thought: What do you think low valence and high energy means?

Low valence High energy ???

All you need to do is pay attention to your valence and energy.

Is this a research-based approach?

Yes! The approach used here is based on the RULER method, developed by researchers at the Yale Center for Emotional Intelligence

This approach is being adopted in school districts across the US as part of a push to teach emotional intelligence.

This is also known as a mood meter approach.
